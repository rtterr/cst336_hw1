What is availability?

Availability is defined by the International Organization for Standardization as follows:
property of being accessible and usable on demand by an authorized entity
This means making sure that your data is not obstructed from being accessed.

How it can be threatened

An improperly-maintained system can have access to it disrupted non-maliciously, due to hardware failures or bandwidth issues. Attackers can also force the disruption of service, with a famous method being a Denial of Service (DoS) attack. This overloads a server with numerous access requests, so many that legitimate users are left unable to access or can only do so with degraded speed.
